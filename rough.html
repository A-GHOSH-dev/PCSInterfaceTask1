ABOUT HACKATHON 2k21

A great idea requires a healthy environment to be turned into a reality. E-Cell at IIIT Pune comes forth with its flagship event - HACKATHON 2k21 - a design sprint-like event in which computer programmers or anyone involved in software development collaborate and work on problems that itch their brains. We are glad to offer you an opportunity to connect with like-minded people and combine your enthusiasm levels to come up with authentic software solutions.

THEMES

ANDROID APP DEVELOPMENT

Want to delight android users around you by coming up with intriguing android app solutions to make their lives easier? This is a hands-on experience to explore your potential in the field of android development with intensive coding skills.

FULL STACK WEB DEVELOPMENT

Enough of the user experience! Get onto the other side of the line. Now is a chance to build an effective website environment using all possible languages and frameworks of web development with added business logic and user mindset.

Test your expertise in creating user-friendly interfaces for user-data interaction using front end technologies. Contribute towards a safe as well as intuitive user experience by effectively combining your skills in design, technology and coding.

FAQS

What is the registration fee for Hackathon?

There is zero registration fee for this event.

How to register for the event?

You can register through our website or directly at DevFolio.

What is the format for Round 1 submission.

You can check the submission format here.

When and where will I receive the problem statement?

Problem statement will be mailed to you directly on 18 June 2021 00:00 HRS IST. You can also download the same from this website.

Who can participate in the event?

Anyone enrolled in a UG/PG course in any country is eligible to participate.

How many people are allowed in a team?

You can have a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 4 members in a team.

How long is the Hackathon?

Participants who qualify for Round 2 will be up for a 50 hours Hackathon.

Will you be assigned the theme and problem statement?

You can choose any theme out of the given 3 but the problem statement will be assigned by us accordingly.

What technologies you must/must not use for Hackathon?

There is no restriction on the use of any technology.

Can you choose more than one theme?

No, you are strictly allowed to choose only one theme.

Can you change the theme midway through the Hackathon?

No, you have to stick to the chosen theme throughout the Hackathon.

How many problem statements will be there in one theme?

There will be only one problem statement in each theme.

Is it important to team up with people from your organisation?

No, you can team up with anyone eligible to participate in the event.
